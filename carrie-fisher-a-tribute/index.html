Life of Carrie Fisher

  • 1956 Carrie Fisher was born into a showbiz family on 21 October 1956 – the daughter of Debbie Reynolds and Eddie Fisher
  • 1973 Carrie Fisher’s stage debut was on Broadway in the adaptation of the musical Irene, which also starred her mother
  • 1975 Carrie Fisher’s screen debut came in the movie Shampoo.
  • 1977 Was Cast as Princess Leia in Star Wars: A New Hope.
  • 1983 Singer Paul Simon and Carrie Fisher were married from August 1983 to July 1984.
  • 1987 Publishes her first novel, Postcards from the edge.
  • 2005 Awarded Women of Vision Award.
  • 2006 Writes and performs her one woman show Wishful Drinking.
  • 2016 Dies from a heart attack on a flight from London to Los Angeles on December 27th.
